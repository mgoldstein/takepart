Did World Leaders Finally Tackle Climate Change?

After a marathon 72-hour final push, exhausted delegates to the UN climate change conference in South Africa broke an impasse over how to best combat global climate change by agreeing to... keep talking for four more years.

Still, United Nations officials are heralding the new agreement, known as the "Durban Platform," as a "historic breakthrough to save the planet," two years after the last major climate talks ended in utter failure in Copenhagen. Continue Reading »
